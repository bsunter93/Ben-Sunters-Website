A Little About Me

I'm a Program Manager at Google, but my journey started with a degree in Industrial Engineering. I've always been fascinated by how things work, and now I apply that curiosity to the complex world of digital advertising. My goal is simple: to help publishers thrive in a privacy-focused world. 🚀

Whether I'm leading a multi-billion dollar merger or helping a publisher unlock new revenue streams, I believe in a human-centered approach. It's not just about data; it's about understanding people and their needs.
